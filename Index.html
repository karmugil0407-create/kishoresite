My Story

I am a man of dualities. By day, I am a dedicated Physiotherapist, helping people recover their strength and mobility. Understanding the human body's mechanics is my profession, and I approach it with precision and care.

By heart, I am a Car Racer. The adrenaline of the track, the roar of the engine, and the pursuit of the perfect lap time define my passion.

When I'm not in the clinic or behind the wheel, you'll find me behind a lens. Photography allows me to slow down time and capture the beauty of the machines I love and the world around me.
